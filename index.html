Welcome to Golden Age Aviation

Disclaimer,

Golden Age Aviation is a virtual airline for home flight-simulation enthusiasts. It is not a real-world airline, does not operate aircraft, and does not sell or book flights.

Golden Age Aviation is a non-commercial hobby community that simulates historical airline operations across multiple carriers, including (but not limited to) Lufthansa (DLH), Air India (AIC), Kingfisher (KFR), Jet Airways (JAI), and Singapore Airlines (SIA). Our aim is to recreate schedules, fleets, and procedures from the “golden age of aviation” as closely as reasonably possible for educational and entertainment purposes.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Lufthansa, Air India, Kingfisher Airlines, Jet Airways, Singapore Airlines, or any of their parent companies, subsidiaries, or partners. All trademarks, service marks, logos, and liveries remain the property of their respective owners. Any real-world references (airline names, aircraft types, routes, logos, imagery) are used for historical simulation and identification only. If a rights holder requests modification or removal of material, we will act promptly.

No commercial activity is conducted via this website/community. Membership is voluntary; participation requires a consumer flight-simulator platform and add-ons chosen by each member. All flight activity is virtual.

For the official airlines, please visit their real-world websites:

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Golden Age Aviation ("we," "us," or "our") is a non-commercial virtual airline community dedicated to flight simulation enthusiasts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

This is a hobby community for flight simulation only. We do not operate real aircraft, sell tickets, or provide any real-world aviation services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Username and display name
  • Email address
  • Discord ID and username
  • Pilot callsign (e.g., GAA0001)

Flight Simulation Data:

  • SimBrief user ID
  • vAMSYS pilot profile data
  • Flight logs and statistics
  • ACARS data and position reports
  • Simulator type (MSFS, X-Plane, P3D)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your pilot account
  • Track and display flight activity on our live flight map
  • Generate flight statistics and leaderboards
  • Communicate community updates and events
  • Ensure compliance with community rules

4. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with:

  • vAMSYS - Pilot accounts and flight tracking
  • SimBrief - Flight planning integration
  • Discord - Community communication
  • VATSIM/IVAO/POSCON - Online flying networks

We recommend reviewing each service's privacy policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Flight data may be displayed publicly on our website. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at:- operations@goldenageaviation.org

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for session management and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
